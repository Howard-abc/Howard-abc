### 👨🏿‍💻 **About Me & My Mission**
I am a Data Analytics student at SIUE and an aspiring Health Data Architect. I apply an essentialist philosophy - focusing strictly on "less but better" - to design secure, interoperable data pipelines for the healthcare sector.

Rather than just building models, my mission is to master the architecture and governance of health data: **Data Provenance & Ingestion** → **Transformation & Auditing** → **Governance & De-biasing**. Ultimately, I am building toward attaining global authority in algorithmic accountability and launching a health-tech enterprise alongside clinical expertise.
